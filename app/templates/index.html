Test that a web platform's import feature works. Take a screenshot when complete.

Task
  1. Visit pantheon.io
  2. Click "Create Free Account" and complete the registration form.
  3. You will be taken to your newly created account. Click "Create your first site."
  4. Name your new site and click "Create Site" to continue.
  5. Select "Archive Importer" at the top.
  6. In the upload box, paste "https://pantheon-infrastructure.s3.amazonaws.com/testing/canary2.tgz"
  7. Click "Import Site" to begin process. Wait until you get an error or are taken to a Dashboard.
  8. You will either see an error message or a button that says "Visit your Pantheon Dashboard." Take a screenshot of either the Dashboard or the error message.
You can use a screengrab tool such as jing, skitch, or Nimbus.
